ANI - Anime Duchy

Working on: Strawb
Status: Planned
    Main Gimmicks:
    Monarchism: Uniting OR expanding the subjects
    Democratic:
    Traditionalism: Undo various progressive reforms while
    balancing stability and public support. Progressives will
    start a civil war if the reforms are too drastic.
    Liberalism/Democratic Socialism: Start united, then eventually
    break apart into a Liberal party and a DemSoc party, who initially
    rule as a coalition, but soon you have to decide which to support.
    Liberalism: Reform the Empire further into a (IRL) modern
    liberal democracy, with rights for most citizen and a focus
    on social welfare.
    Democratic Socialism: Rally against the far-right, and
    slowly expand the definition of "far-right" to eventually rule
    as a truly socialist state, which means only one party, but
    under a more gay form of socialism (which is why these guys
    are under "democracy" rather than "communism"). The gimmick in
    this one is that you have a BoP you have to keep down, else
    people will see you as impeding their rights too much and
    overthrow you.
    Communism (Minaism): Devolve the Empire and its fiefdoms into a proto-
    agraro-industrial state, in which nature is practically worshipped. You have
    to balance your harmony with nature and your industrialization with, you
    guessed it, another BoP. Harmony increases things like population growth,
    stability, and lowered consumer goods, but tanks production and construction
    speeds. Industrialism is the inverse. No irreversible effect on maxxing one out,
    this BoP stays with you, and you can do what you want with it.
    Pro-Anime Reactionism (Fascism): The Empire's culture and traditions are seen
    as a source of pride and strength. In reaction to Anti-Anime Reactionism, this path
    will see you prepare for, and ultimately, enact war against the Anti-Anime Reactionist
    nations, and depending on your choice during the focus tree, the human realms in general.

LOL - Loli Duchy

Working on: Planned
Status: Planned
    Main Gimmicks:
    ALL: Expanding into France and Iberia
    Monarchism: Juggling Expansion and domestic unrest by the conquered populations.
    If the expansions get too quick, you tank your stability and war support, and risk
    facing civil wars in the territories. This will be done via a decision category
    rather than BoP, because we will target individual states which contribute to a
    growing "dissatisfaction". Basically, a countdown always ticks down as you have
    non-cored states, and the more you have, the worse the consequences. We track
    dissatisfaction via a numbered variable, which increases on each countdown fulfillment
    and at a certain point, start an uprising, though not a civil war. Look into how to
    do this war so if LOL loses, they only lose those states.
    Democratic: Improve your relationship with France and
    attempt to work together into an eventual unification. If this does not work,
    or the CEU already formed the Anti-Anime Front with France
    in it, OR there has already been a war between France and LOL, you either try
    to force state-wide referendums, or divert to the always-available-but-peace-
    locking War with France focuses.
    Communism (Anarcho-Communism): Seeing the resistance of the French and the
    lack of support from the Empire, you decide to try what some of the cooperative
    Frenchmen have been promoting; A radical form of Communism - Anarcho-Communism.
    After either a slow and peaceful or fast and bloody revolution, you set your
    sights on becoming fully independent and reuniting not only France, but also
    retaking previously French territory from the Empire. If
    you had a fast and bloody revolution, the Queen is killed (retire and set flag).
    If you become monarchist at a later point again, or you fail the revolution, you
    must do a decision that gives you a new Queen, and switches your focus tree.
    Monarchism (post-AnCom revolution): With the old Queen dead and a new
    Queen on the throne, you now have to decide which direction your state goes.
    Will you be loyalist, or will you seek independence? Regardless, you must
    recover from the civil war (add spirit after the war) and restart your economy
    before setting your sights at both fortifying the country and planning your
    takeover of France.
Main Goals:
1. Conquering France and Iberia. Fracturing Spain into multiple subject states if
becoming a "King in the Empire", else get other buffs without independent Spanish
states.
2. Either becoming independent, or becoming a "King in the Empire", keeping loyalty
to the Empire, but having near-total sovereignty. Essentially, cut the "puppet"
relation and instead get a national spirit and an endless truce, as well as a massive
AI strategic goal to be friendly and allied with each other.

NAT - Central Processing Unit

Working on: Planned
Status: Planned
Starting National Spirits:
The Holy Mother (Pre-Realization): Natsuki is at the center of the AI's inner working. Although not de-jure holy, she is equivalent of the AI's Jesus to our Jesus. Unfortunately, all living beings must die one day, and she does not have much time. We must keep this hidden from the AI, lest we invoke agony yet unknown.
An incapable bureaucracy: The Fall of the United States of America has hit New Jersey not with blood and guns, but with an unfixable bureaucracy, which resists any attempt to truly change the core of the nation.
Home of the Innovation: Despite its unassuming past, New Jersey has become the home of innovation - countless technological achievements are invented and discovered here, including the most advanced Artificial Intelligence system ever thought up.

Special Attention National Spirits:
The Holy Mother (Realization) Natsuki is not yet gone. She is missing, and the goal is to find Her. No resource must be spared, no man or woman left unaccounted for, no hands left without work, until we find where She has disappeared to. The False Prophets will not win, Her grace still shines upon us all. The sun turns to Her holy color, and the moon reflects Her holy light. Onmi koso subete, onmi koso hikari! (御身こそすべて、御身こそ光！)!
    Main Gimmicks:
    ALL: Cognitive Stability
    Cognitive Stability:
    Automaton State (-1 to -0.8): Barely sentient at all, many debuffs. Allows "disabling dangerous AI features" focuses (see below).
    Proto-Cognition (-0.79 to -0.4): Can manage small sectors of the state, but still rudimentary. Debuffs still at play.
    Baseline Awareness (-0.39 to 0): Some buffs to production, but still many debuffs.
    Directed Cognition (0.01 to 0.3): Small buffs to production and military, but stability debuffs.
    Integrated Intelligence (0.31 to 0.6): Medium buffs to production and military, but stability debuffs. Can take AI-centric focuses.
    System Hegemony (0.61 to 0.99): Large buffs to production and military, but stability and political debuffs. Can take AI-centric focuses.
    Realization: Instantly removed BoP, switches focus tree to empty. Global news event about "AI shut down in [TAG.GetNameDef]". Fire event some days to a month later, switching to the Natsuki AI and switching to the AI-Insanity Focus Tree.
    ALL: Population Conversion:
    Prerequisite: Either ascend the AI or have the AI relalize. This will be decided when we get development here.
    Step 1: Select a State while in the Decisions tab. An event will fire soon.
    Step 2: Selected state will have its population saved to a temporary variable.
    Step 3: Selected state will have its population set to 0. An event with the temporary variable will be sent soon.
    Step 4: You have a:
    40% Chance to get 1/4th of the population back,
    30% Chance to get 1/3rd of the population back,
    20% Chance to get 1/2 of the population back, and
    10% Chance to get the entire population back,
    along with a state modifier permanently increasing pop growth IF owner is NAT. If it is NOT, a near-catastrophic debuff to anything in the state.
    This modifier can be removed by any non-NAT and non-NAT-puppet state, via a similar method as above.
    This method, however, will delete the population, take a chunk of another state's population, and transfer them over.
    Since you can't print people like you can cyborgs/robots, you do, however, get a nice bonus to your pop growth there. Of course, if the state is reconquered by NAT, this bonus will be removed, and NAT will have to convert the state again.

    Democratic (Start): Starting with the New Democratic-Republican Party in power, you generally first do economic and research focuses first, while befriending your Northern, Western, and Southern neighbors appeased. This path is intended to end once you either hit Realization, or you manage to disable all dangerous AI features and switch to J.U.N., UNDECIDED, or UNDECIDED.
    Pro-Anime Reactionism (UNDECIDED):
    Neutrality/Isolationism (J.U.N.): instead of forcing laws that punish people who don't support AI-overlordship, you improve their lives through freely choosable focuses until you hit a certain, decision-displayed threshold, which unlocks more focuses. Ultimately, you attempt to initiate referendums in the other U.S.A. nations, either giving you a state close to you, or increasing a decision-displayed value which, when hitting `num of states / 2`, causes a civil war, which NAT can join on the side of the rebels, and if won, they get annexed. If the rebels capitulate, NAT whitepeaces the defender and gets a temporary debuff. If the AI support focuses also unlock decisions to make people happy, you get a large decrease in AI support, which has to be counteracted using either more focuses or, when ran out of focuses (or just for it), the decisions.
    Communism (UNDECIDED):
    Post-Consolidation: After conquering the entire North American continent, you get a new focus tree and eventually "cool down" and seek friendly relations with either Japan, China (if reunited and a reasonable ideology), or ANI/CEU (post-Anime War).
Main Goals:
Reunite the United States of America to at least its past borders.

CSA - The Southern States

Working on: Planned
Status: Planned
    Main Gimmicks:
    Start (Democratic): The declining KKK and the rising Amerikadeutscher Volksbund are facing conflicts with each other in the new, democratic South. Tensions wane, and the two factions regularly clash (via decision and a random-list function), pushing their conflict (Balance of Power) into either direction. The President needs to carefully balance his own allegiance, favoring the KKK or the ADVB at various points to secure his own power and perhaps take the two organizations' power away.
    Neutrality (KKK, Oligarchism): Should the KKK become too powerful, they will initiate a civil war in Mississippi, Georgia, Alabama, S. Carolina, N. Carolina and EITHER Tennessee or Florida with several buffs. After winning the war, they will switch to a KKK focus tree which focuses on curtailing non-Anglo American influence, re-uniting the USA under the banner of the KKK (which will switch to some other flag after uniting), and becoming an isolationist superpower. After uniting the USA, they will switch from Oligarchism to Isolationism and generally try to stay uninvolved in the Anime War.
    Neutrality (DAVB, Despotism): Should the DAVB become too powerful, they will initiate a civil war in Texas, Arkansas, Louisiana, Virginia and EITHER Tennessee or Florida with significant buffs. After winning the war, they will switch to a DAVB tree, which sees them decide on Pro-, or Anti- Anime Reactionism, in which they both become NatSoc. They will have focuses to invite various Nazi leaders to join them, and the ability to aid the Anime War. Their ultimate goal will be to unite the USA and become expansionist, seeking to establish a "guiding hand" over the Americas. If you invite the right people, you might even get Hitler into power.
    Democratic: Should the president be able to abolish the KKK and the DAVB, he establishes a more progressive system and can choose between regular Liberalism, Democratic Socialism, or to push Communism (Marxism).
    Liberalism: In order to ensure the continued non-existence of the two racialist parties, and prevent the creation of new ones, the president pushes forward revolutionary policies such as "giving women more rights" or "maybe we should permit non-Whites some more rights". Basically, an early Civil Rights movement, but supported by the president.
    Democratic Socialism: The president, intent on pushing racial equality, promotes a form of Socialism while keeping it democratic. For this, he pushes much more progressive ideas than in the Liberalism route, tanking stability while greatly increasing industrial output and recruitable population (of course, with some debuffs because people don't easily switch from traditionalist to progressive).
    Marxism: Refer to TFR's California.
Main Goals:
1. Reunite the USA.

Glossary

Gscheadmadl - Made-up Bavarian word for "Tomboy" - "Gschead" meaning "rough, uncouth"; "Madl" meaning "girl". Plural: Gschertmedla.
Zwünch - Rarely used online term for "Twink". Plural: Zwinch.
Schpottgea - Made-up Bavarian word for "Mesugaki" - "Schpott" meaning "Mocking"; "Gea" ("Göre") meaning "brat". Plural: Schpottgera.
Feldhelda - Made-up Bavarian word, can mean "Field Heroes" or "Field Holders". Singular: Feldheld (Field Hero), Feldhalda (Field Holder).
Laibgade - Bavarian word for "bodyguard" and a version of "Leibstandarte". Plural: Laibgada.
